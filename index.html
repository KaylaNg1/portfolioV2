a bit about me


Hello, hello! My name is Kayla Ng and I am a sophomore studying Computer Science at Cornell University's College of Engineering. I first discovered my passion for Computer Science during my freshman year of high school when I took my first CS class ever: Intro to Visual Basic. Ever since, programming has become my creative outlet, a way for me to bring to life all of the ideas that are constantly bubbling in my brain. I love the challenge of coming up with successful algorithms and debugging. There is no greater feeling than seeing my code come to life. Outside of academics, I am a huge book reader/collector. In my free time, you can always find me reading a book or trying to find good deals at my favorite local used book stores. I also enjoy art and love to sketch and paint, specifically characters of my favorite shows which include Avatar the Last Airbender and One Piece.
